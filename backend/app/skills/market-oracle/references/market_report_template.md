## 1. Executive Summary
- Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM) with headline number
- Growth rate and key driver
- Top 3 trends shaping the market
- Primary opportunity identified

## 2. Market Definition & Scope
- What is included/excluded from this market
- Geographic scope
- Product/service categories covered
- Time horizon for analysis

## 3. Market Sizing
### TAM (Total Addressable Market)
- Top-down estimate with source
- Bottom-up calculation showing work

### SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
- Filters applied (geography, segment, pricing)
- Resulting market size

### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- Realistic 3-5 year capture with market share assumptions
- Growth trajectory

### Market Growth
- Historical CAGR (3-5 years)
- Projected CAGR with key growth drivers
- Risks to growth trajectory
